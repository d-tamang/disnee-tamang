About Me

Hi there! Thanks for taking the time to browse my personal site. A little background about myself - I grew up in Albany, CA and attended college at the University of California, Santa Barbara where I earned a B.S. in Environmental Studies. I went on work in tech sales for an ag-tech startup based in Oakland, CA where my curiosity about the technology I was introducing to potential clients and the method of delivery to them sparked a desire to delve into software engineering. I attended App Academy, a rigorous 16 week coding bootcamp in San Francisco, CA and left with not only the technical knowledge to pursue a career in the field, but also a genuine passion for creating. I love the process of bringing an envisioned concept to life via coding.

My current skills include: Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React/Redux, PostgreSQL, SQL, MongoDB, Express.js, Node.js, HTML, CSS, Git, & AWS S3.

LooseLeaf Tea

LooseLeaf Tea is a single-page e-commerce clone of a locally based tea company, Leland Tea, built primarily with React, Redux, Rails, PostgreSQL, and AWS S3. Users can browse the site's collection of teas, create an account, leave reviews, and add items to their cart.

TheftDeflect

TheftDeflect is a single-page site where users can create an account and report/view instances of Bay Area-based, vehicle-related crime occurrences. It is built in cooperation with 3 other developers using MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js, and Google Maps API.

Hungry Brain

Hungry Brain is a simple math game designed for children that enchances addition and multitasking skills. It is built just using Vanilla JS, HTML5, and CSS3.
